ABOUT US

Combined experience of more than 35 years

Ingress Employment Solutions is a specialist recruitment agency for international employment within United States of America and Canada. We offer a wide range of employment options for individual as well as mass hiring. We offer personalized support for your business according to your company’s specific requirements. Our professionals will meet with your HR department at your office to discuss your hiring possibilities, and they have the expertise to formulate an efficient plan that will allow you to realize your primary recruitment goals. As a global recruitment agency, we source applications from candidates from all over the world. We constantly screen new resumes and categorize applicants according to their skills and experience to ensure that we help employers meet their desired applicants from the global talent pool.

The Solution to Your Employment Needs. We have an expert team of professional recruiters who have many years of experience providing the most comprehensive solutions for international recruitment and job placement.

We have access to the most extensive global database of qualified job seekers, which allows us to find the most skilled employees from Jamaica, Honduras, Brazil, Dominican Republic, Belize, Philippines, Mexico, Nicaragua, Sri Lanka and Puerto Rico.
